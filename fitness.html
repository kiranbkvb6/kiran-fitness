BARBELL BENCH PRESS
4 SETS 6 REPS HEAVY
What it does to your body

Primary driver of pectoral (chest) hypertrophy. Recruits pectoralis major, anterior deltoid, and triceps simultaneously. The horizontal pressing pattern thickens the mid and lower chest giving you that defined, full chest projection CR7 carries. Heavy bench press also triggers high testosterone and growth hormone release — your primary fat-burning hormones.

NON-NEGOTIABLE INSTRUCTIONS
  • Arch your lower back slightly — keep glutes and upper back on bench
  • Bar path: touches lower chest (nipple line), not collarbone
  • Grip width: slightly wider than shoulder width (index fingers on rings)
  • Unrack with locked elbows, lower slowly (2 seconds down)
  • Drive feet into floor during the press — full body tension
  • Do NOT bounce the bar off your chest
Advantages for YOUR goals

At 97.4 kg with 38.8 kg muscle mass, you have the frame to press serious weight. This directly attacks your chest fat pockets and builds the defined pec line visible at 12–15% body fat. Expect to bench 90–100 kg within 6 months.

What it does to your body

The hip thrust is the most direct glute-isolation compound exercise. Research shows it activates the gluteus maximus more than any other exercise. Strong glutes are your body's largest muscle group — they are your primary fat-burning engine, protect your knees and lower back, and create the powerful athletic stride. NON-NEGOTIABLE INSTRUCTIONS

Every single meal must contain a protein source. Skipping protein at any meal triggers muscle breakdown (catabolism) within hours. Your 165g daily protein is non-negotiable for muscle preservation during fat loss.

SLEEP IS YOUR HORMONE THERAPY
7–8 hours of sleep is where 80% of your growth hormone is released. Poor sleep = cortisol spike = fat retention and muscle loss. No supplement replaces sleep. Lights off by 10:30–11 PM. No screens 30 min before bed.
📈
PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD EVERY WEEK
Every single week, attempt EITHER +2.5 kg on compound lifts OR +1 additional rep per set. Without progressive overload, you maintain — you don't grow. Track your lifts in a notebook or phone every session.
🚫
NO EGO LIFTING
Your connective tissue was sedentary for 6 years. It does NOT recover at the same speed as muscle (muscle memory). Tendons and ligaments take 3–6 months to catch up. Ego lifting is the fastest way to a 3-month injury setback. Form first, always.
🦵
PROTECT YOUR KNEES
At 97.4 kg, your knees carry 3× body weight during squats and walking. Spend 10 min warming up lower body before leg day. Use knee sleeves if needed. Never lock out knees on leg press. Stop immediately if you feel sharp knee pain (not muscle soreness — that's different).
📸
PROGRESS PHOTOS WEEKLY
Take photos every Sunday morning (same lighting, same pose). Scale weight fluctuates daily by 1–2 kg due to water and food. Photos are the only true measure of body composition change. Do NOT judge progress by scale alone.
🧂
ELECTROLYTES WITH WATER
With 10 km daily walking + gym, you lose significant sodium, potassium, and magnesium through sweat. Add a pinch of pink Himalayan salt to 1–2 glasses of water daily. Low sodium causes fatigue, headaches, and poor gym performance that you'll mistakenly blame on sleep or diet.
🍚
DO NOT FEAR CARBS
You perform 10 km walking + 1 hr gym daily. You NEED 270g of carbohydrates. Carbohydrate restriction at this activity level destroys performance, causes muscle loss, and slows metabolism. White rice is your performance fuel — not your enemy.
🔄
SUNDAY = MANDATORY REST
Rest day is a TRAINING DAY for your nervous system, tendons, and hormonal recovery. Overtraining is real — it is characterized by declining strength, poor sleep, irritability, and fat retention. Honor your rest day as much as your training days.
🎯
FAT LOSS IS TOTAL BODY — NOT LOCAL
Belly crunches do NOT burn belly fat. Abdominal exercises build the muscle UNDER the fat. The fat is lost systemically through caloric deficit + hormonal optimization + training. Your program does all three. Trust the process, not spot-reduction myths.
